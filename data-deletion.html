Effici Data Deletion Requests

This page is for the Effici app and explains how to request deletion of your data. It is intended for users visiting from the Google Play listing.

Option A: Delete account and all app data

  • Open the account deletion flow and submit your account email.
  • Confirm via secure email magic link.
  • Your Effici account and associated app data are permanently deleted.
Delete account and all data

Option B: Delete some data and keep your account

If you want to keep your account but remove specific data (for example sessions from a date range), send a deletion request email.

  • Send email to whynkay@gmail.com with subject: Effici Data Deletion Request.
  • Include your Effici account email and what should be deleted (e.g. sessions/projects/tags/date range).
  • We may ask you to verify account ownership before processing.
  • Target processing time: within 30 days.
Request partial deletion by email

What data is deleted or retained

Data type Deleted when requested What may be kept and for how long
Effici account identity (auth user/profile) Deleted for full account deletion requests. May be retained only where required for legal/security obligations.
Productivity data (sessions, distractions, projects, tags, stats, timer settings) Deleted for full account deletion, or selectively for partial deletion requests. Encrypted backups/logs may persist temporarily for up to 30 days.
Crash/security/operational logs Not always tied to direct account deletion workflows. Retained only for reliability/security purposes for a limited period (typically up to 90 days).
Billing transactions (Google Play) Not directly deleted by Effici (managed by Google Play). Retained by payment provider per legal/accounting requirements.
