**Last Updated**: February 15, 2026

## Introduction

Effici ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Effici mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

**By using Effici, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email Address**: Required for account creation and authentication
- **Password**: Stored securely using industry-standard encryption (managed by Supabase Auth)

### 2. Productivity Data

When you use the App, we collect and store:
- **Pomodoro Sessions**: Start time, end time, duration, completion status
- **Projects**: Names and descriptions of your work projects
- **Tags**: Custom tags you create to categorize sessions
- **Distractions**: Notes about interruptions during focus sessions (if you choose to log them)
- **Session Descriptions**: Free-text descriptions of what you worked on
- **Daily Statistics**: Aggregated data about your productivity (total focus time, session count, etc.)

### 3. Device Information

We may automatically collect:
- **Device Type**: Android device model and manufacturer
- **Operating System**: Android OS version
- **App Version**: Version of Effici you're using
- **Device Identifiers**: Advertising ID (for ad serving)

### 4. Usage Data

We may collect information about how you use the App:
- **App Activity**: Features used, screens viewed, navigation patterns
- **Session Duration**: Time spent in the app
- **Crashes and Errors**: Diagnostic information when the app crashes (if crash reporting is enabled)

### 5. Advertising Data

If you use the free, ad-supported version of Effici, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect:
- **Advertising ID**: Used for personalized advertising
- **Ad Interactions**: Which ads you view and click
- **Device Information**: For ad targeting and fraud prevention

## Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

### Encryption
- **In Transit**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **At Rest**: Your password is hashed using bcrypt; session data is stored securely in encrypted databases

### Access Controls
- Limited access to production data (only authorized personnel)
- Two-factor authentication for administrative access
- Regular security audits of our infrastructure

### Cloud Infrastructure
- Data hosted on secure, enterprise-grade servers (Supabase)
- Regular backups to prevent data loss
- DDoS protection and firewall rules

**However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.** While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Privacy Rights

### Data Access & Portability
You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your personal data stored in the App (via Settings → Account)
- **Export**: Download your productivity data in CSV format (via History → Export)
- **Portability**: Take your data to another service

### Data Correction
You can:
- Edit your account information (Settings → Account)
- Modify or delete individual sessions (History → Long-press session)
- Update projects and tags (inline editing)

### Data Deletion
You have the right to request deletion of your data:
- **Account Deletion**: In-app Account screen → Delete account
- **What Gets Deleted**: All productivity data, projects, tags, session history, and your account
- **Timeframe**: Immediate local deletion; server-side account and data deletion is initiated immediately after request
- **Cannot Be Undone**: Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered

### Advertising Control
You can:
- **Opt Out of Personalized Ads**: Device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **Remove Ads Entirely**: Upgrade to Effici Premium (in-app purchase)

### Additional Rights (For EU/UK Users - GDPR)
- **Right to Object**: Object to processing of your personal data
- **Right to Restriction**: Request restriction of processing under certain conditions
- **Right to Lodge a Complaint**: Contact your local data protection authority

## Data Retention

### Active Accounts
- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- Productivity data is stored indefinitely (until you delete it)
- No automatic deletion of old sessions

### Deleted Accounts
- Local data deleted immediately upon account deletion
- Server-side account and associated data deletion is initiated immediately
- Backups purged within **90 days**
- Legal obligations may require us to retain certain data longer

### Inactive Accounts
- Accounts inactive for **3 years** may be flagged for deletion
- We will email you 30 days before deleting an inactive account
- You can prevent deletion by logging in
